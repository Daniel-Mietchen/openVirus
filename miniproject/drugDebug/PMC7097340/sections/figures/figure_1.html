Antiviral drugs. The antiviral drugs are used for HIV (human immunodeficiency virus), herpes viruses, influenza A and B viruses, and the HBV (hepatitis B) and HCV (hepatitis C) viruses. Some of the commonly prescribed antiviral drugs are given. NRTI, nucleoside reverse transcriptase inhibitor; NNRTI, non-nucleoside reverse transcriptase inhibitor; PI, protease inhibitor
