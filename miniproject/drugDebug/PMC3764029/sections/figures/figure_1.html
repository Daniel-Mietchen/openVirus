Growth of HCV in the presence of 5-Fluorouracil. J6/JFH1 and HCV-RR2 viruses were serially passaged in Huh7D cells in medium containing the indicated concentration of 5-Fluorouracil. At each passage HCV titers were obtained as described in the text. Titers are expressed as the mean number of foci of each of four replicates. Error bars represent the standard deviation.
