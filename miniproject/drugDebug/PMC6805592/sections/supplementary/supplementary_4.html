Additional file 1.

Confocal image analysis and quantification after RV or UV-inactivated RV infection of mouse PCLS. (a)-(d) Tissue slices were stained with 4 μM calcein AM and 4 μM EthD-1 after 48 h of submerged cultivation. Images were examined by confocal laser scanning microscopy and analyzed with IMARIS. Green color shows the cytoplasm of vital cells and red color shows dead cell nuclei (diameter of 4 μm). (e) n = 3 independent experiments with three analyzed images per slice (duplicate slices per condition) (One-way ANOVA). Scale bar: 500 μm.
