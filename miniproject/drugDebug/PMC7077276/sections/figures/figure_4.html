( d, e) EGCG eluted dcapsid protein bound to a heparin column with similar efficiency as heparin. Heparin-Sepharose HP column was pre-loaded with dcapsid and eluted with 0.05 ( d) or 0.5 ( e) mg/mL soluble competitor reagents (heparin, EGCG and EC).
