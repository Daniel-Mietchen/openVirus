Cytotoxity of the seven Thai medicinal plant extracts on MARC-145 cells determined by the 3-(4,5-dimethylthiazol-2-yl)-2,5-diphenyl tetrazolium bromide (MTT) assay. MARC-145 cells were incubated with various concentrations of these plant extracts or control without plant extract for 72 h prior to the MTT assay. Values are expressed as mean ± standard error. CN, Clinacanthus nutans; PF, Perilla frutescens; HC, Houttuynia cordata; TT, Tiliacora triandra; CS, Caesalpinia sappan Linn.; GM, Garcinia mangostana Linn.; PE, Phyllanthus emblica; CC 50, 50% cytotoxic concentration
