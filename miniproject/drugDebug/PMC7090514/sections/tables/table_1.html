IFN-α Nebulization: using with caution in neonates and infants younger than 2 months

Nebulization: 200,000–400,000 IU/kg or 2–4 μg/kg in 2 mL sterile water, twice daily for 5–7 days

Spray: 1–2 sprays on each nostril and 8–10 sprays on the oropharynx, once every 1–2 h, 8–10 sprays/day for 5–7 days

 Contraindication: CrCl < 50 mL/min; histories of mental illness, severe or unstable heart disease, or aplastic anemia
