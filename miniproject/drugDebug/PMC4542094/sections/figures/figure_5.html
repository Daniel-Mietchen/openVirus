Isobologram showing the in vitro interaction between amantadine and doxorubicin, cisplatin or 5FU in HCT8 WT and HCT8 RETO cells. Dose-response curves are based on the respective IC 50 values calculated after 24 hours of incubation. Amantadine-doxorubicin are synergistic in HCT8 WT/RETO ( a & b). Amantadine-cisplatin are additive in HCT8 wildtype ( c) and synergistic in HCT8 RETO ( d). Amantadine-5FU are synergistic in RETO ( f) but antagonistic in HCT8 WT ( e). n = 3 independent experiments
