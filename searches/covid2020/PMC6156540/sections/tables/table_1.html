NCT02283099; NCT02296983; NCT02287480 Phase I; Phase I/II
Nov 2014 158 VSV, attenuated
single dose IM 3 × 10 5, 3 × 10 6, 1 × 10 7, 2 × 10 7, 5 × 10 7 PFU Antigen: GP EBOV (1995) 		Safety:
Doses of 1 × 10 7 PFU or higher:
- Arthralgia in 22% (11/51) participants of Geneva cohort; arthritis confirmed in 9/ 11 cases; maculopapular rash in 27% (3/11) of these cases
- Self-limiting cases of arthritis in 3.4% (2/60) participants in Germany and Kenya cohort Dose of 3 × 10 5 PFU:
- Reduced adverse events in mild to moderate range with arthralgia in 23% (13/56) participants
Immunogenicity:
- Antibody responses in all subjects; persisted for 6 months
- Dose dep. VNTs in 85% (107/126) of vaccinees
