Abstract

Liquid silicone (polydimethylsiloxane) is an inert material that is commonly used for cosmetic purpose. We describe a case of severe silicone pneumonitis complicated with acute respiratory distress syndrome and bilateral pneumothorax secondary to silicone gluteal augmentation.
